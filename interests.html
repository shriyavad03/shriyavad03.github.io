Hailing from a family of writers, I have always had a keen interest for good writing of all languages- be it song lyrics, stories, poetry, or even film-scripts! I write about anything that piques my interest.

Kuchipudi

Having learnt Kuchipudi for over 11 years, dancing has been one of my most personal forms of self-expression. I believe Kuchipudi has helped me stay grounded, confident and taught me the most about myself.
